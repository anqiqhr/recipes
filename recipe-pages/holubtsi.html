Holubtsi

Description

Holubtsi, or stuffed cabbage rolls, is a classic Ukrainian dish made of cabbage leaves wrapped around a flavorful mixture of rice and meat.

Slow-cooked in a tomato-based sauce, it’s a hearty and comforting meal enjoyed by many families.

Ingredients

For the Rolls:

  • 1 medium cabbage
  • 1 lb (450 g) ground pork or beef
  • 1 cup (200 g) cooked rice
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sause:

  • 2 cups (500 ml) tomato juice or purée
  • 1 cup (250 ml) water or broth
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Boil the cabbage whole for 5–7 minutes, peel off the leaves, and trim the thick stems.
  2. Mix ground meat, cooked rice, onion, egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Place 1–2 tbsp of filling on each cabbage leaf, roll tightly, and tuck in the edges.
  4. Place rolls in a pot, pour tomato sauce mixed with water and butter over them, and simmer for 1–1.5 hours on low heat.
  5. Serve warm with sour cream and fresh herbs.
